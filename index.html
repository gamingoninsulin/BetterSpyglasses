What is Better Spyglasses?



Better Spyglasses is a plugin that's adres ver OP Spyglasses.
Now you are wondering what van you do with this plugin. I shall explain!
With this plugin you can make special kinds of spyglass(es) that wil spawn the corresponding ores.


You need 8 pieces of the ores list. And 1 normal spyglass

    Ore List:

  • Coal
  • Lapis Lazuli
  • Quartz
  • Copper Ingot
  • Iron Ingot
  • Gold Ingot
  • Diamond
  • Emerald
  • Netherite Scrap
